History

TechCon was founded in 2010 with the aim of bringing together technology enthusiasts, innovators, and industry leaders. Over the years, it has grown into one of the premier technology conferences, attracting thousands of participants from around the world.

Our mission is to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and empower the next generation of tech leaders. We strive to provide a platform for sharing knowledge, showcasing cutting-edge technologies, and connecting industry professionals with emerging talent.
